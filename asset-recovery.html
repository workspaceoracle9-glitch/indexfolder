Asset Recovery & Compensation

Tracing stolen assets and returning them to victims.

Asset Recovery Process

The Office of the Director employs advanced forensic accounting and digital tracking technologies to trace assets that have been illicitly acquired or hidden by criminal enterprises. Our dedicated Asset Forfeiture Unit works tirelessly to identify, freeze, and seize these funds.

  • Tracing: Utilizing international banking cooperation to follow the money trail.
  • Seizure: Obtaining court orders to freeze bank accounts and seize physical assets.
  • Forfeiture: Legal proceedings to transfer ownership of illicit assets to the government for restitution.

Victim Compensation

Our ultimate goal is not just to seize assets, but to return them to the victims of fraud.

Once assets are forfeited, the Remission process begins. This involves identifying eligible victims and calculating their pecuniary loss. The Department of Justice then authorizes the distribution of funds.

How to File a Claim

If you believe you are a victim of a financial crime investigated by our office, please contact the Victim Assistance Unit immediately.
