About The Director

"Our mission is clear: to safeguard the nation's financial infrastructure and ensure justice for victims of fraud."

Welcome to the official portfolio of the Office of the Director. It is my distinct honor to serve as the 28th Director of the United States Secret Service.

In an increasingly digital world, the threats we face are evolving. From complex wire fraud schemes to transnational organized crime, my office is dedicated to staying one step ahead. We are committed to not only apprehending those who seek to do harm but also to recovering assets and returning them to their rightful owners.

Leadership in Financial Crimes

Spearheaded the formation of the Global Cyber Fraud Task Force, resulting in the recovery of over $500 million in stolen assets.

Western Union Settlement

Played a pivotal role in the historic $586 million settlement, ensuring compensation for thousands of victims worldwide.

FBI Recovery Operations

The Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI), in conjunction with the US Secret Service, holds the primary authority to investigate federal financial crimes.

Our Asset Recovery Unit is tasked with the identification and seizure of assets derived from criminal activity. We operate under federal forfeiture laws which allow the government to take ownership of property used to commit crimes.

Asset Recovery & Compensation

The Process

The Office of the Director employs advanced forensic accounting to trace assets illicitly acquired by criminal enterprises.

  • Tracing: Following the money trail.
  • Seizure: Freezing bank accounts.
  • Forfeiture: Transferring ownership to the government.

Victim Compensation

Our ultimate goal is to return assets to victims. Once forfeited, the Remission process begins to identify eligible victims and calculate loss.
