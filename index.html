Case File: The Abandoned Shipment

Discovery

An anonymous tip led Director Curren's team to a remote warehouse where a shipment of high-value medical equipment was found abandoned.

The Connection

Forensic analysis linked the shipment to a shell company operating out of Eastern Europe. Collaboration with Interpol confirmed the link.

Resolution

After a 6-month investigation, the shipment was released to its rightful owners, a humanitarian aid organization, ensuring critical supplies reached those in need.

Director on Duty

Western Union Settlement: $586 Million Returned

Director Sean M. Curren played a pivotal role in the historic agreement with Western Union to forfeit $586 million to resolve charges of aiding and abetting wire fraud.

This fund is now being used to compensate victims of fraud schemes who used Western Union to transfer money to scammers. Below are some of the beneficiaries who have successfully received their settlements through our office.
