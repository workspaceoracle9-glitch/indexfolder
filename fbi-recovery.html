FBI Recovery Operations

Our authority, process, and commitment to justice.

Our Role & Authority

The Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI), in conjunction with the US Secret Service, holds the primary authority to investigate federal financial crimes. Our Asset Recovery Unit is tasked with the identification and seizure of assets derived from criminal activity.

We operate under federal forfeiture laws which allow the government to take ownership of property used to commit crimes or acquired with the proceeds of crimes.

The Recovery Process

1
Investigation

Agents gather evidence to link assets to criminal activity.

2
Seizure

Assets are frozen or physically seized via court warrant.

3
Forfeiture

Legal proceedings transfer title of assets to the US Government.

4
Restitution

Funds are distributed to victims or used for law enforcement purposes.
